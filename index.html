About

Finger Trips is a DJ and producer based in Łódź, Poland, and the founder of Potok — a house music series running since 2020.

His sound blends minimal, deep tech and classic house influences, shaped by early inspirations from funk, soul and disco.

He has released music on labels such as Miura Records and Berlin House Music, and regularly performs at club P29.
